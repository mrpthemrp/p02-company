About The Company

Posterized Kicks was founded on April 20, 2021 by Noah De Guzman, a passionate sneaker enthusiast who wanted to bring the art and culture of the sneaker world into his own living space. His desire for high-quality, exclusive artwork inspired him to create Posterized Kicks - a company dedicated to providing customers with the best possible sneaker posters. Each poster is carefully designed and printed on premium quality paper to ensure that every detail is captured perfectly, making them unique and special.

What started as a hobby soon became a thriving business as more and more people discovered the beauty and value of Posterized Kicks' exclusive posters. At Posterized Kicks, we are proud to continue Noah's legacy by providing our customers with exceptional sneaker art that celebrates the unique design and culture of the sneaker world.
