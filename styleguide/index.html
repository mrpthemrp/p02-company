Basic Branding Ideas

What does the company do, or offer?

PosterizedKicks is a company that specializes in collectible and tradeable sneaker posters. They offer a wide range of posters featuring some of the most popular and iconic sneaker models from brands like Nike, Adidas, New Balance, and more. Their posters are high-quality, printed on thick, cardstock paper, and feature vibrant colours and crisp details.

Who is the company to potential customers?

PosterizedKicks is a company that caters to sneaker enthusiasts and collectors who are looking for unique and high-quality posters to display in their homes or offices. The company is for people who take interest in fashion, especially sneakers, and have an eye for design and style. They are passionate about their collections and they want to showcase their appreciation for their favorite sneaker designs in a visually appealing way. Customers are looking for a company that can offer them the opportunity to own a piece of sneaker history, and PosterizedKicks offers them that opportunity.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

It offers a unique and visually appealing way to display a person's passion for sneakers. The company's high-quality posters are designed to be framed and displayed, allowing customers to showcase their favorite sneaker designs in a decorative and stylish manner. The company's unique focus on sneaker enthusiasts and collectors and the high-quality of their products and services may also attract customers. Because each poster is very limited, this added value of rarity and collectibility makes the posters more desirable and valuable to the customers.

User Persona

Name: Michael
Age: 26
Occupation: Graphic Designer

Michael is a graphic designer with a strong interest in fashion and sneakers. He's been collecting sneakers for over 10 years and is always looking for new ways to display his collection. He often visits sneaker-related websites, blogs and social media channels. He's an avid art collector with an eye for design and style, and is drawn to unique, visually appealing items to add to his collection. He discovered PosterizedKicks through a sneaker forum and was impressed by their high-quality posters with popular sneaker designs and customization options. As for every sneaker fan out there, Michael has a strong interest in collectibles and limited edition items and believes that PosterizedKicks posters will be perfect for his collection and for display.

Why did I choose this colour palette?

I chose a colour palette that is neutral and minimalistic. This colour palette includes shades of white, gray and black, and compliments of blue, creating a clean and modern look that would be well suited for showcasing the posters.

How will it affect the users?

A neutral and minimalistic colour palette can create a clean and modern look for the website, making it easy for users to navigate and focus on the main content, which in this case are the posters. This type of colour palette can create a sense of simplicity, elegance and timelessness, making the website look professional and trustworthy. It can also convey a sense of simplicity and functionality, which can be beneficial for a website that sells a specific product, as it allows the user to focus on the product itself rather than being distracted by a lot of colours and elements.

Potential imagery, and, or, art direction

Customized posters: Showcasing the customized posters in a visually appealing way, such as on a wall, in aframe, or on a desk, can be used to convey the company's customization options.

Sneaker design sketches: Showing sneaker design sketches and illustrations can be used to create a sense of the creative process behind the posters and the company's attention to detail.

Sneaker macro shots: High-quality, detailed macro shots of sneakers, shot in a studio or on location, can be used to showcase the posters and the attention to detail that goes into them.
